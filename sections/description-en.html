Competency Questions back to ToC

This section shows the specification of the AffectedBy ODP requirements in the form of Competency Questions:

  • CQ1: What are the qualities that influence a feature of interest?
  • CQ2: What are the qualities that affect a given quality of a feature of interest?
  • CQ3: Which feature of interest does a given quality belongs to?

Instantiating the AffectedBy ODP back to ToC

This section shows an example of instantiating the AffectedBy ODP. Let us consider a problem that deals with ensuring the thermal comfort of a lecture room located in the ground floor of a building. This room has a large window that overlooks the road that passes near the building and some qualities of the room include its area, the number of seats available or the quality of comfort at any given time. The quality of comfort in this room is affected by the room temperature and the nearby outdoor noise. In turn, the temperature of the room is affected by the number of people present in the room, the humidity of the room, and the intensity of the solar radiation received through the room window. Regarding the impact of the outdoor noise, it is affected by the sound insulation factor of the room. Lastly, the received solar radiation is affected by the azimuth (i.e. orientation) of the room window.
